A Quick Bio

Nothing makes me happier than coming in from a day of shooting photographs and watching my computer fill with small thumbnails of captured beauty. In that moment, it feels like walking through a library - my mind overwhelmed by potential. I chase that feeling and love sharing my final products with whomever will look at them.

I wasn't always this way. I grew up in a typical Texas home that exalted sports. It wasn't until my senior year of college that I bought my first camera. Since then, photography has pulled me all over the world serving as a catalyst to great experiences and friendships.

I'm currently traveling around North America chasing the right seasons and locations to grow my portfolio. You can follow my attempts on my live map page which shows my most recent photo I felt worthy of sharing, on instagram, or watch my latest drone video on Youtube.
