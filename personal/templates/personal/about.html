A Quick Bio...

Born and raised in suburban Dallas, Texas, Cody grew up mostly disconnected from nature. It took one trip to Oregon to inspire Cody to move from his hometown to explore, create and experience more of the natural world in the Pacific Northwest. What started with car-camping and weekend trips has turned into a globe-wide journey.

Cody actively contributes to various photographic communities, attends workshops and interacts with anyone willing to inquire on Instagram. While not photographing, Cody is usually programming websites or practicing yoga.
